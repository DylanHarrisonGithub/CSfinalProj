Instructions

This game requires two players! If you don't have an opponent, navigate to this site in a second browser tab.
Once both players have clicked ready, the game begins.
When it is your turn, click the 'shoot mode' button, then click where you want the cue ball to go to take your shot.
While in 'pan mode' click to center the screen and use the mouse wheel to zoom in or out.
